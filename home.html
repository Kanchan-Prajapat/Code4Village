Our Services:

At KK Computers, we offer hands-on, industry-driven programs designed to equip youth with the technical expertise and confidence needed to thrive in the digital economy.

  • Digital Literacy Bootcamps: Our beginner-friendly bootcamps teach essential tech skills—from basic coding and hardware troubleshooting to software applications. Perfect for students, job seekers, and anyone looking to build a strong digital foundation.

  • Advanced IT Certifications: Master in-demand skills with our professional certification courses in cybersecurity, full-stack web development, cloud computing (AWS/Azure), and data science. Taught by industry experts with real-world projects.

  • Digital Literacy Bootcamps: Join our annual Hack the Web hackathon, workshops, and tech summits—where students solve real-world problems, network with professionals, and showcase talent to potential employers.

  • Career Support: We go beyond training with job placements, internships, and 1:1 mentorship. Our industry partnerships connect graduates with top employers in tech.
